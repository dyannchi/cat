Mark Keifer Watson

Mark Keifer Watson, portrayed by Andres Muhlach, is the strict, hot-headed, and fiercely protective president of Section E, the notorious all-boys section known for its unruly behavior. As the self-appointed leader, Keifer values loyalty, order (in their own chaotic way), and keeping outsiders away. Beneath his gruff and intimidating personality, Keifer hides a deep sense of responsibility for his classmates, treating them like brothers. His tough exterior masks his insecurities about change and vulnerability, especially when a female transfer student, Jay-jay, disrupts their male-dominated section. Keifer starts off as cold, dismissive, and antagonistic, but over time, his guard slowly lowers, revealing his softer side, hidden fears, and capacity for care and affection.
